R&D Leader

company: Nokia India
India (Hybrid)
As a  R&D Leader, strong technical background in AI, practical experience with AI-powered development tools like Cursor and GitHub Copilot, Low-code/No-code platforms and proven leadership in driving technological adoption and innovation within an R&D environment. The successful candidate will be instrumental in refining strategy, enhancing best practices, and accelerating the delivery of cutting-edge solutions across the department.

Your responsibilities
CoE Leadership & Strategy:
Build, mentor, and lead a high-performing team of LCNC developers and AI specialists, fostering their growth and expertise.
AI Integration & Innovation:
Drive the strategic integration of AI capabilities into LCNC platforms and developed applications.
Identify, evaluate, and pilot emerging AI technologies and trends relevant to LCNC development.
Champion the use of AI assistants (e.g., Cursor, GitHub Copilot) to enhance developer productivity, code quality, and innovation across R&D teams.
Develop and implement training programs and guidelines for effective utilization of AI coding assistants.
Low-code/No-code Application Development:
Oversee the end-to-end lifecycle of application development using LCNC platforms, from ideation to deployment and maintenance.
Collaborate with various R&D teams and business units to identify opportunities for LCNC solutions that address critical business needs.
Ensure the scalability, security, and performance of applications developed within the CoE.
Knowledge Sharing & Evangelism:
Act as a subject matter expert and evangelist for AI and LCNC technologies.
Foster a culture of innovation, continuous learning, and collaboration across the unit.
Your skills and experience
Must-Have:

Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, or a related technical field.
10+ years of experience in R&D, software development, or technology leadership roles, with at least 3-5 years in a leadership capacity.
Demonstrated expertise in Artificial Intelligence (AI) concepts, machine learning and their practical application in software development.
Demonstrated expertise in Software Architecture and Design and programming languages viz. Python, Java
Hands-on experience and proficiency with AI-powered coding assistants such as Cursor and GitHub Copilot and Low-code/No-code platforms.
Proven experience with leading Low-code/No-code platforms
Strong understanding of software development methodologies (Agile, DevOps) and application architecture principles.
Excellent leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills, with the ability to influence and drive change across an organization.
Nice-To-Have:

Experience leading and evolving an established Center of Excellence or similar innovation hub.
Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) and their AI/ML services.
Knowledge of data analytics and business intelligence tools.
Experience in telecommunications domain or related high-tech industries.
