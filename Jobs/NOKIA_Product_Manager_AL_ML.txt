Job description
Product Manager AI/ML is a senior product management role focused on defining and managing an AI/GenAI development platform. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of AI/GenAI technologies, experience with product management in a technical environment, and the ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.
HOW YOU WILL CONTRIBUTE AND WHAT YOU WILL LEARN
• Define and communicate the strategic vision for AI/GenAI development platforms aligned with organizational goals.
• Develop a dynamic roadmap for platform evolution, prioritizing features based on emerging AI trends and team feedback.
• Responsible for setting the platform's vision, managing its lifecycle, and ensuring its alignment with business objectives.
• Oversee the complete product lifecycle, from concept through delivery, ensuring quality and timely updates.
• Provide technical guidance on development best practices and support a range of AI activities from data processing to deployment.
• Lead user training initiatives and create comprehensive documentation to enhance platform proficiency among internal teams.
• Monitor platform performance continuously and iterate on enhancements based on user feedback and technological advancements.
• Foster effective communication across teams to encourage platform adoption and drive collaborative success.
